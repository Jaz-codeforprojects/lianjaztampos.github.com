About Us

The People

Seventh-Day Adventists believe all people are created equal in the eyes of God and that we were created equal in God's Image. Because of This, it is vitally important help people understand the Bible to find healinf, hope and freedom in Jesus. Only through personal relationships with Jesus can each of us grow into our full potential as human beings whether we are children, women or men.
Our church is not about having four walls and a pulpit. it is about living in harnony as the body of Christ. This means each church member loves, respect and values the other members, realizing that just as different organs make up the body, so different, so different members are needed to form the Church. Sure, we'll disagree sometimes, adn from time to time, even the closest family have some friction. That's when Christian love and patience comes in. As Christians we have the privilege to model for the world how God calls us to live in a caring, compassionate community. Here's to a happy family with Jesus at the center!
